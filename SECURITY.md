### What to Include

1. Clear description of the vulnerability.
2. Steps to reproduce (Proof of Concept).
3. Potential impact and threat scenario.

---

### Our Response Process

1. Acknowledge receipt within **48 hours**.
2. Investigate and assess severity.
3. Communicate a remediation timeline.
4. Publicly credit the reporter (unless anonymity is requested).

---

## Out of Scope

Unless they directly compromise seed generation or derivation, the following are generally out of scope:

* Attacks requiring physical access (Evil Maid).
* Social engineering attacks.
* Denial-of-Service (DoS).
